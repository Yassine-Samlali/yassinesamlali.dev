This application was built to help hotel teams manage reservations and room status through a structured interface.

## Features

- Room availability and status management
- Reservation registration and updates
- Customer information management
- Simplified operational workflow

## Objective

The objective was to make daily hotel management faster, clearer, and more reliable.
