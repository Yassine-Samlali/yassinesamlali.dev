This web app was developed to simplify pharmacy operations by organizing products, stock levels, and daily sales in one place.

## Features

- Product and category management
- Stock monitoring with low-quantity alerts
- Sales registration and history
- Basic reporting for operational follow-up

## Objective

The main objective was to improve organization and reduce manual errors in daily pharmacy management tasks.
