This project focuses on helping teams manage inventory efficiently with clear visibility into incoming and outgoing products.

## Features

- Product in/out tracking
- Supplier and stock records
- Inventory status overview
- Faster lookup and filtering for items

## Objective

The goal was to centralize stock data and support better operational decisions through a practical web interface.
